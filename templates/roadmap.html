Fighter Roadmap

Follow the ultimate training path to become a champion.

Phase 1

Basics & Fundamentals

Learn the fighting stance, core footwork, and balance. AI monitors your posture ensuring you build the correct muscle memory without bad habits.

Phase 2

Striking Mechanics

Master the jab, cross, hooks, and uppercuts. Focus on speed, snap, and kinetic linking from your legs through your hips and into your fists.

Phase 3

Core & Cardio Conditioning

Develop the gas tank required for a 5-round fight. High-intensity interval training tailored precisely to combat sports stamina generation.

Phase 4

Defense & Evasion

Slips, rolls, parries, and blocking. The AI tracks head movement speed and reactivity so you can avoid incoming strikes gracefully.

Phase 5

Advanced Combinations

Link strikes gracefully into terrifying combos. Understand pacing, range management, and how to set traps for your opponent.

Phase 6

IronJaw Elite Challenge

Test all your skills into a multi-staged AI sparring crucible. Real-time feedback pushes your limits as if you have a world-class corner.
